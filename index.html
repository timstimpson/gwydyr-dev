The Salon

A friendly relaxed light-hearted professional experience, we have been described as a proper barbers and feel that you will understand this once you come in and sit down, relax and soak up the honest and real feel of one of the oldest barber shops around.

Gwydyr Hairdressing Saloon is one of the oldest barbers in the South of England.

With our knowledge and experience we are able by to offer a wide choice of modern or traditional styles for hair and beard that suit you and your needs. Gwydyr pride ourselves in being able to offer a top level service to all ages young, old and the busy bit in between.

Our Story

I started in the shop back in September 1991 on a YTS scheme which included two days a week at Crowns hairdressing academy where I studied practical and the theory of hairdressing. The rest of the week I’d be sweeping the floors, making tea and folding towels. Slowly working up to shampooing clients, tapering hairlines and eventually to cutting hair. I thank my father and fellow master barbers teaching me the skills which I have used at Gwydyr ever since.

Imported barbering chairs from Chicago circa 1930’s.

My father who owned Gwydyr before myself started in April 1957, he wanted a week off before he started his apprenticeship at Gwydyr but was told if he wanted to work here then he started when he was told . After a long weekend of freedom having finished school on the Friday he started on the Tuesday of the Easter week of 1957 . Working his way up to eventually owning the business years later.
